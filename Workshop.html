About This Workshop

Prof. Dirk Koch

Novel Computing Technologies · Universität Heidelberg · Institut für Technische Informatik (ZITI)

dirk.koch@ziti.uni-heidelberg.de

Prof. Yoshiki Yamaguchi

Laboratory for Innovative Custom Logic Architecture (LILA) · University of Tsukuba

yamaguchi.yoshiki.ge@lila.cs.tsukuba.ac.jp

The REACT Workshop brings together researchers from Universität Heidelberg (ZITI — Novel Computing Technologies) and the University of Tsukuba (LILA — Laboratory for Innovative Custom Logic Architecture) to share recent advances in reconfigurable computing and edge AI semiconductors, and to lay the groundwork for sustained international collaboration between the two institutions.

The workshop provides a forum to discuss emerging research directions, explore joint project opportunities, and build personal connections that can seed future exchanges of students and researchers.
